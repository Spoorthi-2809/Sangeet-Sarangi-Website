About Sangeet Sarangi

"Sangeet Sarangi Keyboard Classes offers professional keyboard training for kids and adults.

Learn Bollywood songs, basic to advanced keyboard techniques, chords, rhythm, and music fundamentals in a simple and enjoyable way.

Classes are available both online and offline (at home sessions in Bangalore). Whether you are a beginner or want to improve your playing skills, personalized guidance is provided to help you play confidently. Special focus on Bollywood songs, practical playing, and performance skills. Enroll today and start your musical journey with Sangeet Sarangi."
